# Crypto Talent Finder

## Here to help enable our customers in to pay for Fintech professionals through Ethereum blockchain. Making the task of finding the right professional for your needs simple and painless.

### Project Summary
#### In this application, we are assigned as Lead Developer for Crypto Talent Finder, where we will build a front-end web application and allow our clients to choose a fintech professional and seamlessly pay for their services with Ethereum. There are a list of different candidates with various rates for their services. Dependent on your needs and how much you're will to pay, you can select from this list the appropriate professional.

### Technoligies
* Python
* Ethereum
* Web3.py
* Streamlit
* Bip44
* Dotenv is used to manage user credentials
